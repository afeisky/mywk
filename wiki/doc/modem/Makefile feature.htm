-----------------------------------------
（1）__2G_CAP_TURN_OFF__
       作用：当手机的Preferred network type选择“WCDMA only”，手机发起GMM_ATTACH_Request时，在UE上报能力中，
                  将Access Technology Type和RF Power Capability设置为000.  参考e-service:ALPS02709017

  目前Pixi5-5 4G的测试情况，手机发起GMM_ATTACH_Request消息如下：
  0001 .... = Access Technology Type: GSM E --note that GSM E covers GSM P (1)

            .... 1010  000. .... = Length in bits: 0x50 (80)

            ...1 00.. RF Power Capability, GMSK Power Class: 2 W (33 dBm) (4)

（2）__ADD_NEW_LTE_RF_SUPPORTED_BAND__
        配置LTE band27，需要添加此配置，参考e-service:ALPS02721520
